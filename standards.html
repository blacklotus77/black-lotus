NON-NEGOTIABLE

1. AUTHORIZATION

Written authorization required for all engagements. Scope, targets, and timeframes defined in contract. No authorization, no operation. Out-of-scope assets are never touched.

LEGAL BOUND

2. LEGAL BOUNDARIES

No black-hat work. Ever. No extortion. No destruction. No unsanctioned access.

Client vetting mandatory. We reserve right to refuse service.

Findings disclosed to client only. No public disclosure without remediation + consent.

Compliance required. Cybercrimes Act of SA, CFAA, GDPR, local law.

FRAMEWORK

3. METHODOLOGY

Testing follows documented frameworks:

NIST SP 800-115 - Security Testing Guide

OWASP WSTG - Web Security Testing Guide

ENCRYPTED
