// Standards

The code we operate under. No exceptions.

1. Rules of Engagement

Every engagement requires explicit written authorization defining scope, targets, and timeframes. No authorization, no engagement. We will not test assets outside the signed scope.

2. Legal & Ethical Boundaries

  • We do not engage in black-hat activity. No extortion, no data destruction, no unsanctioned access.
  • We do not work with criminals. All clients are vetted. We reserve the right to refuse service.
  • We report all findings to the client only. No public disclosure without remediation and consent.
  • We adhere to all applicable laws. Cybercrimes Act of South Africa, CFAA, GDPR, and local regulations.

3. Methodology

Our testing follows industry-standard frameworks:

  • PTES - Penetration Testing Execution Standard
  • NIST SP 800-115 - Technical Guide to Information Security Testing
  • OWASP - Web Security Testing Guide

All findings include proof-of-concept, business impact, and actionable remediation steps. No vague "vulnerability found" reports.

4. Data Handling

  • Client data is encrypted at rest and in transit.
  • Exfiltrated data during red team ops is used for proof only, then securely deleted post-engagement.
  • We retain reports for 90 days unless otherwise specified. NDAs honored indefinitely.

5. Recruitment

Degrees optional. Proof of work mandatory. If you can build, break, or defend systems at a high level, we want to talk. Send GitHub, tool writeups, or CTF credentials.

We do not hire for illegal work. If your first question is about carding or ransomware, you will be blocked.

6. The Creed

We are the people. We are the signal. So We Stand.

This means we stand between those who cannot defend themselves and their adversaries. We use offensive knowledge for defensive purpose. We do not flip sides.
